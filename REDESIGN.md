# Spilled Palette Studio - Website Redesign

## Artist: Aswathi Bindhu Jawahar

### Target Audience
- Art enthusiasts and collectors
- Interior designers
- Home decorators
- Gift buyers

### Design Principles
1. **Art First** - The artwork is the hero, not the website
2. **Clean & Elegant** - Minimal distractions
3. **Professional** - Trust-building for buyers
4. **Easy Navigation** - Find art by category/mood quickly

### New Structure

#### 1. Hero Section
- Artist name prominently displayed
- Artist statement/tagline
- Featured artwork carousel
- Clear CTA to view gallery

#### 2. About the Artist
- Aswathi Bindhu Jawahar bio
- Photo of artist
- Artistic philosophy
- Brief background

#### 3. The Gallery
- Filter by category: Botanical, Floral, Sanctuary, Modern, etc.
- Grid layout with hover for in-situ
- Click for larger view
- Price display
- "Add to Cart" or "Inquire" button

#### 4. Why Buy Original Art
- Value proposition section
- Testimonials (if available)
- Shipping/returns info

#### 5. Contact/Connect
- Contact form
- Social links
- Newsletter signup

### Visual Style
- **Colors:** Warm, neutral palette (alabaster, soft charcoal, terracotta accents)
- **Typography:** Elegant serif for headings (Playfair Display), clean sans for body (Inter)
- **Spacing:** Generous white space
- **Images:** High-quality artwork photos, in-situ views on hover

### Navigation
- Logo/Home
- Gallery
- About Aswathi
- Contact

### Key Features
- Responsive design
- Fast loading
- SEO optimized
- Easy artwork filtering
- In-situ preview on hover
- Contact form for inquiries
